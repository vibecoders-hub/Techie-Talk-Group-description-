⚖️ LAW AND ORDER!

💬 Respect and Behavior

  • Don’t hurt others’ emotions with sensitive comments while discussing.
  • Be respectful — behave how you’d want others to treat you.
  • Never assume you’re the smartest one or judge others — everyone learns differently.
  • Be friendly and helpful to all members.

🚫 Content and Restrictions

  • No inappropriate stickers, GIFs, or media sharing (based on team guidelines). Violation = instant removal without excuses.
  • Never discuss non-tech-related or controversial topics.
  • No personal fights, gossip, or drama.
  • Avoid slang, harsh, or abusive language.
  • Never share personal or confidential details in the group.

🧍‍♂️ Activity and Participation

  • Inactive (ghost) members will first receive a notice to engage. If still inactive, removal follows.
  • Participate in discussions, share updates, and help others when possible.
  • If you have an idea, topic, or mini-project — feel free to start a discussion!

🚷 Discipline

  • Inappropriate content or language = multiple warnings.
  • Repeated offenses = temporary or permanent ban, based on behavior and chat history.
